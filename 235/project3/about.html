Project 3: Mini-Golf

Concept:

The plan is to create at least 5 levels of mini-golf in increasing difficulty. Rules are the same as mini-golf and players aim to get the levels completed in the least amount of shots. If there is enough time, I plan to add things like boosters or bumpers to make the game more interesting (final product has rough grass and ice areas instead).

Genre:

Sports and Puzzle

Platform:

Desktop web

Story:

There is no story. Just a casual mini-golf game.

Aesthetics:

A top-down, colorful, and simple design. I want it to feel like the old mini-golf flash games.

Gameplay:

Use your mouse and left click to get the ball in the hole in as little hits as possible. There are 5 levels total and your goal is to make it through them with a score under par.

  • Light Green = Normal grass that will not affect your ball in any way.
  • Dark Green = Rough grass that will slow down your ball.
  • Blue = Ice that will stop your ball from slowing down. Make sure to aim properly before going over ice as it you won't be able to stop on it!
  • Brown = Walls that you can not pass through and will bounce off of.

Controls:

  • Move Mouse: Turns your golf club opposite of the mouse position (the ball will travel AWAY from your mouse). The further away your cursor is, the more power you get.
  • Left Click: Hits the ball in the direction and power specified. You may only hit your ball once it has come to a complete stop. The opacity of the club displays this.
