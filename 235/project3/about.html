Project 3: Mini-Golf

Layout:

Controls:

  • Move Mouse: Turns golf club towards mouse position
  • Left Click: First click sets club position, then the second click hits the ball at a power based on how far away the mouse has moved from the original position
  • Right Click: Ends the locked club position and returns the player to aiming the club

Gameplay:

The plan is to create at least 5 levels of mini-golf in increasing difficulty. Rules are the same as mini-golf and players aim to get the levels completed in the least amount of shots. If there is enough time, I plan to add things like boosters or bumpers to make the game more interesting.
